About Me

I am a self-proclaimed Pokemon Master, Digidestined, and Planeswalker, and I'm passionate about exploring the deep lore of the worlds I grew up watching and playing. My love for storytelling extends beyond the screen as a LARP staffer, roleplay gamer, and aspiring author. Living by the creed of "Being the person I needed when I was younger," I strive to create worlds and tell stories that resonate with people who might not have anywhere else to look.
